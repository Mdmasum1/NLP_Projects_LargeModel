   ###Most Popular NLP book
There are many books available on natural language processing (NLP) and building NLP models, and the best one for you will depend on your background and the specific topics you are interested in. Here are a few books that are highly regarded in the field and may be worth considering:

    "Speech and Language Processing" by Daniel Jurafsky and James H. Martin: 
This is a comprehensive textbook on NLP that covers a wide range of topics, 
including syntactic and semantic processing, language modeling, and 
machine translation. It is suitable for students and professionals with 
a background in computer science or linguistics.

    "Natural Language Processing with Python" by Steven Bird, Ewan Klein, and
 Edward Loper: This book is a practical introduction to NLP using the Python
 programming language. It covers a wide range of topics,
 including text processing, language modeling, and machine learning, 
and includes exercises and examples to help you practice and apply what 
you have learned.

    "Deep Learning for NLP" by Jason Brownlee: This book is a comprehensive 
guide to using deep learning for NLP tasks, including language modeling, 
machine translation, and sentiment analysis. It is suitable for readers with a 
background in machine learning and some familiarity with NLP.

These are just a few examples, and there are many other great books 
available on the subject. It may be helpful to do some research and read 
reviews to find a book that is well-suited to your needs and interests.
